Full-Time Vacancies at Africa AI (5 Positions)

Africa AI – Posted by Sunlit Centre Kenya –  Nairobi, Nairobi County, Kenya

Job Description

We are a fast growing startup building Africa’s largest impact data labeling workforce by empowering underserved communities to join the digital economy and break the cycle of poverty. Founded in 2021

Quality Analyst

Responsibilities:

  • Analyze taskers’ work output against guidelines/standards and providing feedback
  • Coaching taskers and helping them improve on their weaknesses
  • Providing feedback to Team Leaders on identified areas that individual members of the team need to improve on for coaching
  • Compiling and sending daily, weekly and monthly quality reports and send to the Quality Analyst Supervisor and other relevant stakeholders
  • Collaborating with Operations to ensure that best standards are revised and updated where need be
  • Providing feedback and recommendations on recurring and widespread gaps that should be addressed through training/refresher trainings
  • Maintaining an up-to-date record of individual taskers’ quality performance for use in performance reviews

Requirements:

  • Great attention to detail
  • Excellent communication and feedback skills
  • Planning and organization skills
  • Strong interpersonal skills
  • Reporting and analysis skills
  • Conflict prevention and resolution skills

Senior Project Manager

Responsibilities:

  • Manage overall performance and governance of the Statements of Work
  • Manage the day-to-day operations in accordance with the requirements and SLAs set out in this SOW
  • Prompt identification and resolution of service delivery issues including implementation of preventative measures
  • Flag and escalate business risks timely to the client
  • Transition management during the setup phases or expansion phases
  • Establish the vendor infrastructure necessary to perform the services, including all technology, financial, human resources, security, facilities, and communication resources.
  • Install all Service delivery processes and Service Level reporting mechanisms
  • Provide exceptional  mentorship and career development to your team to achieve low attrition levels and high employee engagement
  • Drive operations teams to deliver continuous improvement and productivity/quality gains

Requirements:

  • Dynamic leader with experience managing large teams across multiple sites in a fast-paced environment
  • Stellar attention to detail
  • Excellent communication and feedback skills
  • Reporting and analysis skills
  • Conflict prevention and resolution skills
  • 4+ years of experience in operations, preferably in the technology industry
  • 2+ years of people management experience, including managing leaders, and a strong desire to develop team members
  • Bachelor’s degree, or equivalent

Director of Finance and Operations

Responsibilities:

  • Oversee the financial strategic planning of the company by evaluating its performance and potential risks.
  • Develop a data warehouse and automate KPI’s across business to improve efficiency and effectiveness.
  • Perform regular financial analysis, develop budgets, and conduct variance analysis.
  • Lead DFID and Challenge Fund grants, reporting and program management
  • Monitor and track personnel and payroll data to provide relevant management information.
  • Recognize patterns in company spending, revenue, and recommend solutions to any problem areas.
  • Improve efficiency by eliminating unnecessary reporting and implementing new streamline controls and reporting for AP/AR and payroll.
  • Design and implement oversight tools that enable departments to reconcile charge processing to schedules and monitor daily productivity.
  • Develop full suite of financial and cost accounting procedures to ensure timely and accurate reporting of information to operations and owners.
  • Create and implement automated accounts payable process and procedures resulting in a more efficient, accurate and timely product delivery system.

Requirements:

  • Bachelor’s degree in accounting, finance, or relevant field.
  • A minimum of 5 years experience in financial management.
  • In-depth knowledge of accounting software, corporate principals, and financial analysis.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal abilities.
  • An analytical mind with good organizational skills.
  • Outstanding leadership skills.
  • Ability to think independently and motivated.
  • Experience at an early stage tech startup or proven track record operating in the earliest stages of a company.

 

Director of Learning and Development

  • Thrive in a fast-paced startup environment with ambiguity
  • Strong passion to upskill underserved workers and provide more opportunities to communities in emerging markets in Africa
  • Entrepreneurial drive to build a team from the ground up
  • Working closely with development and delivery teams to ensure high quality onboarding, training, and upskilling of workers

While your responsibilities will vary depending on the priorities for a particular quarter, here is a sampling of what they will include

Responsibilities include:

  • Be a critical part of the leadership team & strategic decision making
  • Lead strategy and execution for learning and development for the entire organization
  • Improving and overseeing processes for worker onboarding, training, and upskilling
  • Ownership of LMS, including selection of LMS platform, content creation, internal roll-out and upkeep
  • Create new strategic initiatives to further develop our workforce and the underserved communities we operate in
  • Build out delivery team capabilities and optimize new workstreams
  • Spearhead grant partnerships and expansion into new geographies
  • Vetting and onboarding new sites and distributor partners for new regions
